The care of every single detail

In a special frame as that of the Amalfi Coast, it's the small details that make us fall in love and that turn a stay into an unforgettable experience.

 

The villa, the interior

The villa has been recently renovated into a style which evokes the Mediterranean architecture, modern and refined at the same time. Its delightful interiors fully preserve the typical charm of the Amalfi Coast's houses, with the famous pottery and ceramics revisited from a modern and tasteful viewpoint, without renouncing to the comfort also in order to satisfy the most demanding customers' desires.

The comfort you expect

During your stay, you will have the daily cleaning of your rooms guaranteed and the cleaning of the garden on alternate days, the use of the Wi-Fi in the swimming pool and in the wellness area, too, satellite TV, air conditioning, lift and sound system with a free App to be downloaded on your mobile phone and, for the winter season, heating system.
