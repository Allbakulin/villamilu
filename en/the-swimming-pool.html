The swimming pool, in detail

Two stairways of access lead to the terrace where the wide 110 squared metres swimming pool is located, equipped with two shower cabins, sunbeds and sofas where you can admire the coastal landscape in the most absolute quiet. You will be able to have lunch under the gazebo shadow or you can also choose to taste a drink at sunset, thanks to the well furnished bar zone, while listening to your favourite music transmitted by an excellent sound system.
