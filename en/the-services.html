The services included in the stay

 

Lift

Ground floor, sleeping zone and balcony of coverage.

 

Wi-Fi

Complete inner coverage; the external coverage is available for the sauna terrace, swimming pool terrace, lunch balcony.

 

Sound System

Available in the living room, swimming pool and wellness area. It can be activated through a free App for smartphones and tablets.

 

Satellite TV

Living room.

 

Air conditioning

In all bedrooms and in the living room.

 

Radiators

Available in every environment for the heating during the winter season.

 

Daily cleaning

It lasts around three hours.

 

Garden cleaning

On alternate days.

 

Usages

Water, electricity and gas consumption.

 

Linen change

Twice weekly.

 

Swimming pool cleaning

Daily.

 

Safe

On the wall, with electronic combination.

 

Animals entrance

Small-size animals are admitted.

 

Welcome to the babies

In the villa, a cradle, a changing table and a high chair are available.

Further services are avaiable upon request

 

Additional daily cleaning

Carried out in the morning and in the afternoon, for the garden and swimming pool's cleaning, and for the linen change, too.

 

The spa, wellness room

The spa includes the use of the wellness room provided with sauna, steam bath, shower and a related space fitted out with chairs and sunbeds.
