Bedrooms, in detail

Nine beds in total, divided in four rooms. A double bedroom at the ground floor, with a comfortable sofa-bed, toilet in the room, a little terrace and a safe; a two-bed room and two double bedrooms at the first floor; one of these has got a private bathroom.

 

Passing from the daytime

Open your eyes towards a shining panorama and begin your day enjoying the benefits of the sunbeams.

to the night time

When it's the time for the "goodnight", turn off the lights and admire the starry sky; then let the night breeze caress you.

Bathrooms, in detail

In the night area, we find an internal bathroom inside the main bedroom, equipped with Jacuzzi in order to allow oneself a further moment of relax and privacy; moreover, we can also find another bathroom which is in common with the other two bedrooms. At the ground floor two other bathrooms; one of them is inside the double bedroom. Every bathroom is equipped with a washbasin, hairdryer and shower cabin.

Do not renounce to your favourite dishes, prepared in your own way

The kitchen, in detail

In order to improvise a fast meal after a day at the seaside or to satisfy your table guests with more sophisticated dishes, perhaps through the collaboration of a home cook, in the kitchen you will find a kitchenette with an aspirant cloak included, microwave oven, dishwasher, refrigerator and ice machine.

 

A refined atmosphere for your convivial moments

The living room, in detail

The living room is conceived to comfortably host up to ten people, for lunchtime or dinner, to watch a film on the 50-inch satellite TV or to listen to your favourite music through our sound system, enjoying the fresh air conditioning during the summmertime or the romantic atmosphere of the fireplace during the winter.
